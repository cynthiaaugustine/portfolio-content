My Product Design Handbook

Hey there! My name is Cynthia I created this website to share my experiences as a Product Designer and the lessons I've learned as a MSI student at the University of Michigan. You can also check out podcasts and books I'd recommend as well some of the design principles I follow. This is a great resource for anyone wanting to get into User Experience design. I know how overwhelming it can be to break into the industry with so many resources – I’ve been there. So I’ve selected some of the resources that have helped me to grow as a designer.

I’m a multidisciplinary designer that leads with empathy, strives for seamless digital experiences, and has an appetite for social impact initiatives. My background in Digital Media and Informatics has shaped me into an open-minded individual and also allows me to bring a fresh perspective to my design process.
